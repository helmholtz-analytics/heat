We are organizing a Heat workshop this November at Jülich Supercomputing Centre and are looking forward to your participation!
This will happen immediately after the [tutorial](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/jsc/news/events/training-courses/training-courses-2026/massive-data-processing) (Don't forget to register for the tutorial as well).

While the tutorial will give a general in-depth introduction into Heats, the following hackathon is focussed on actually implementing Heat in your workflow.
We will have a few short talks by users of Heat and then get to coding.
Jump-start your transition to Heat-based distributed computing by coding it up together with the developers!
Missing some features?
Get it on our radar for future releases right there.

A more detailed program will follow shortly, but please already mark this in your calendar!
We are looking forward to meeting you there!
